## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Senior Product Designer** with 4+ years of experience designing digital products across **SaaS, Web, Mobile, and TV platforms**.

I believe AI doesn't replace designers—it removes the repetitive work so we can spend more time solving real user problems.

My work combines **product thinking, UX strategy, research, design systems, and AI** to build products that are intuitive, scalable, and genuinely useful.

Currently, I'm building AI-powered products while exploring how designers can leverage modern AI tools to move faster without sacrificing quality.
